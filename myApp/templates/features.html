Home Features

Live Video Classes

Participate in real-time video classes with your teachers and classmates. Our platform integrates seamlessly with Google Meet to offer interactive and engaging live sessions.

Assignment Submissions

Submit assignments directly through the platform, track submission deadlines, and receive feedback from your teachers. Manage all your academic tasks in one place.

Quizzes

Engage in quizzes to test your knowledge and track your progress. Quizzes are designed to be interactive and provide instant feedback to enhance learning.

Polls

Participate in polls to provide feedback, vote on classroom decisions, and engage with your peers. Polls offer a quick way to express opinions and gather insights.

Discussion Forums

Engage in meaningful discussions with your peers and instructors. Our discussion forums facilitate collaborative learning and knowledge sharing.
