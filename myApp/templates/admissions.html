General Admission Requirements

To apply for admission, please ensure that you meet the following requirements:

  1. Completed Online Application Form
  2. Scanned Copies of Academic Records
  3. Personal Statement
  4. Recommendation Letters
  5. Proof of Identity (Passport, ID Card)
  6. Recent Passport-sized Photograph

Program-Specific Requirements

Depending on the program you are applying for, additional requirements may include:

  1. Previous Coursework in Related Subjects
  2. Portfolio (for Creative Programs)
  3. Interview with Faculty Members
  4. Standardized Test Scores (e.g., SAT, ACT)

For more detailed information on specific programs, please visit the Courses page.
