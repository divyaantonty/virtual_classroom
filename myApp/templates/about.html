Why Academics Works

Academics is established by a team of education and technology leaders with the vision of revolutionizing online learning.

The platform is designed to provide an interactive and user-friendly virtual classroom experience, quickly attracting attention from educational institutions and corporate training programs.

Personalized Learning

In our Academics, personalized learning empowers students by offering customized lessons and resources that align with their strengths and areas for growth, fostering a more engaging and effective learning journey

Parent Details

As a parent, you can easily monitor your child's grades across all subjects, review performance trends, and receive detailed feedback from teachers to stay informed about their academic progress.

You can also access and manage your child's class schedule, keeping track of upcoming lessons, live sessions, and any changes to the timetable. The platform allows you to track assignment submissions, deadlines, and feedback, helping you manage your child's academic tasks and ensure that assignments are completed on time.

Additionally, you can view and download study materials provided by teachers to ensure your child has all the necessary resources for effective learning. Furthermore, the system enables you to schedule and manage meetings with your child's teachers to discuss their progress, address any concerns, and collaborate on strategies to support their educational journey.
